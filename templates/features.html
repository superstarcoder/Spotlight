Our Sevices

Home Services

Community Driven

Our entire website is completely driven by our community, and their submissions. We do however make sure that all submissions are moderated, to ensure quality of life.

Relevancy

We make sure to recommend you music that you might actually enjoy, whilst supporting smaller creators. This is very important to our website, because we make sure that we are benefiting the creator, aswell as the user of our app.

Ease of Access

Our program maintains a very easy user interface to suggest, and play songs. By using the spotify API, we can play music directly on the browser, allowing users to not have to sort through many programs just to see what was recommended to them.

Spotify API

We directly connect to the spotify api to integrate directly to your spotify account, making the entire process as simple as possible.

Open Source

Our entire project is open source, allowing for other people to add other functionality, and to help maintain our project. This allows us to directly interact with our users, get bug reports, and new features directly with our project.

Mutual Benefit

This website was designed for both sides to benefit. While the user gets new music to listen to, smaller artists get their name shown to the world, thus gaining more attention. We do not gain anything from this website, and all of our source code can be viewed at: https://github.com/superstarcoder/Spotlight

Find some new, and original music from creators that you've never even heard of!

By using Spotlight, you can help small artists and creators get the recognition they deserve.
